AlbuGierke Environmental
Solutions, LLC


AlbuGierke Environmental Solutions, LLC, is an environmental consulting company with a specialty in groundwater and environmental issues with computer science capabilities. We do projects that require a highly specialized yet broad skillset for government entities, municipalities, contractors and private landowners and we take pride in our work. Founded in 2017, AlbuGierke Environmental Solutions, LLC is a HUBzone Woman Owned Small Business.


Our Services


  • Data collection, analysis and interpretation
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Stream and wetland restoration
  • Hydrogeologic, wetland and vegetation mapping
  • Contaminant mapping
  • Database creation and management
  • GIS analysis
  • Stable isotope geochemistry
  • MODFLOW model execution
  • Presentation of technical and training material
