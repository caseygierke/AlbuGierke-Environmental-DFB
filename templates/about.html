AlbuGierke Environmental Solutions, LLC

AlbuGierke Environmental Solutions, LLC, is an environmental consulting company with a specialty in groundwater and computer science capabilities. We take pride in our work and we do projects that require a highly specialized yet broad skillset for government entities, municipalities, contractors and private landowners. Founded in 2017, AlbuGierke Environmental Solutions, LLC is a HUBzone Woman Owned Small Business.

Services Include:

  • Environmental monitoring
  • Stream and wetland restoration
  • Hydrogeologic, wetland and vegetation mapping
  • Contaminant mapping
  • Data analysis and interpretation
  • Database creation and management
  • GIS analysis
  • Stable isotope geochemistry
  • MODFLOW model execution
  • Presentation of technical and training material

Computer Capabilities:

  • Python
  • MySQL
  • SQL Server
  • QGIS
  • Grass GIS
  • MATLAB
  • R
